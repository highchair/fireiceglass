Fire & Ice Studio’s design work is unique in the Architectural Art Glass world. The softening effects of light coming through etched and carved plate glass and mirror creates tranquility in our busy world — a nurturing sea of light and calm.

Our studio art falls into three categories: Anthropological, Natural World, and Geometric. All of these categories draw from Deborah’s education, fascination, universal Feng Shui principals, and a focus on fostering balance, curiosity, and tranquility.
